# Storyboard: Monkey Rentals

Video: 2.0 seconds, 60 frames, 30 fps, 1920x1080.

## Scene 1: Premium Logo Entrance (0.0s - 0.3s / frames 0-9)

- Background: warm off-white cream, clean and close to the Monkey Rentals brand.
- The real Monkey Rentals logo appears centered, visually cut out from its light JPEG background.
- Motion: starts smaller and slightly forward-facing, then eases toward camera with a controlled premium scale-up.
- Details: soft grounded shadow below the logo, faint top reflection sweep, no extra text.

## Scene 2: Controlled Energy Hold (0.3s - 1.2s / frames 9-36)

- Logo remains centered and stable.
- Motion: very subtle micro-bounce, more like a polished loading signature than a playful bounce.
- Accent: a thin horizontal glint passes behind the logo using brand orange and petrol blue.
- Finish: soft edge glow in orange and petrol tones, restrained and elegant.

## Scene 3: Unlock Transition (1.2s - 1.7s / frames 36-51)

- Logo gently scales down and moves upward, as if unlocking entry into the product.
- The cream background begins to reveal a simplified Monkey Rentals dashboard underneath.
- The transition stays clean, with no landing page or CTA.

## Scene 4: App Revealed (1.7s - 2.0s / frames 51-60)

- The logo fades out completely.
- A clean dashboard interface is visible: fleet status, payments, vehicles, and booking rhythm.
- The final frame leaves the viewer inside the application, ready for the main panel.
